Privacy Policy for NaviTV Studio Apps

Last updated: July 2026

NaviTV Studio ("we," "our," or "us") operates several mobile and Android TV applications. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our apps.

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information. Our apps are designed to function locally on your device. Any data processed (such as web browsing history in NaviTV Browser) remains on your device.

Third-Party Services

Some of our apps may use third-party libraries for analytics or crash reporting to help us improve the user experience. These services may collect anonymized data such as device type, OS version, and app performance statistics.
